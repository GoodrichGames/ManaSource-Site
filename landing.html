About Mana Source

Gameplay
Combat in Mana Source is highly interactive and euro-inspired. Players must rely on planning and dynamic execution to win.

In the Campaign, a streamlined solo mode ensures that you can focus on playing your character. Every action is balanced for head-to-head competitive play.
Setting
Mana Sourceis a thrilling high-fantasy adventure. It's set in a world where nation has ravaged nation following the onset of a mechanical revolution. Join a scrappy group on the outskirts of civilization. As you venture out, you'll have to explore, solve puzzles, and defeat deadly adversaries if you're going to survive.
Design Philosophy
Light on Core Rules:You shouldn't have to spend hours teaching a game before you can play.

Gradually build complexity: Each class begins with low complexity actions in order to make getting started easy. Over the course of the campaign, players unlock new actions, gather resources, and complete challenges that add layers of strategy. Discover new combos while adapting to every unique encounter thrown your way!

Story is a Feature not a Flavor: Many dungeon crawlers contain only a light, loosely connected narrative. In Mana Sourcethe story, characters, and various twists are a focal point. The story favors a tight, high-quality narrative of over 140,000 words across 20+ scenarios that you'll remember for years to come.
